Preparing for Interviews

13-June-2025


#

It's been over six months. I'm preparing for interview. But keep failing and getting rejected. So for from January, I attended dozens of virtual interview for MERN developer position. Guess what. I failed to secure single offer letter.


When I got graduated in May 2019. Before December 2019 I managed to secure 9 offer letters. Some offer are from Tech giants like TCS, WIPRO and IBM. But now I can't even secure one offer.


W H Y?


I tried to figure out what was the issue. I got more skills. I have years of experience to showoff in resume. And currenty employed at a global tech leader. It should be easy right? The issue is I'm not consistent. Back in 2019, I attended a minimum of 2 to 3 interviews per week. On few days I have attended 3 interview on the same day. I attended more interviews therefore I got more offer. Moreover I was actively preparing and practicing. Which is not the case right now.


Solution: Must actively prepare and practice for interview.
And attend more interviews to improve the change of securing an offer.
